Fruit Smoothie

Description

A healthy and refreshing tropical fruit smoothie loaded with strawberries, bananas, pineapples, and mangos. A great way to visit the islands of Hawaii without having to leave the comfort of your own home!

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 c Strawberries (sans the stems)
  • 1/2-1 peeled Mango (or 1 c frozen mango slices)
  • 1/4 sliced Pineapple (or 1/2 c frozen pineapple slices)
  • 2 cans Coconut water
  • 1/2-1 c Plain yogurt (I prefer Tillamook)
  • 2-3 tsp Honey
  • Ice (measure according to preference)

Steps

  1. Set up blender
  2. Load fruits, yogurt, and honey (in that order)
  3. Pour coconut water
  4. Load ice
  5. Blend to preferred texture. Add/adjust ingredients to taste
